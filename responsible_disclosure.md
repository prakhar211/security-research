# Responsible Disclosure Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a vulnerability related to any research, tool, or detection
published in this repository, please report it responsibly.

**Contact**: security@\<yourdomain\> or via [GitHub Security Advisories](https://github.com/username/security-research/security/advisories)
**PGP Key**: See `keys/pgp.pub` or fetch from keyserver

## Policy

- I practice coordinated disclosure with a standard 90-day window.
- I will NOT publish weaponized exploit code without vendor coordination.
- If you are a vendor affected by research published here, contact me and
  I will work with you on timeline and remediation guidance.
- IOCs published here are for DEFENSIVE use only.

## Scope

This policy covers vulnerabilities discovered through research in this
repository. For vulnerabilities in my employer's products, contact them
through their own disclosure channels.
